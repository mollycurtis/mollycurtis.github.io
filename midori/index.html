01 — Introduction

Who is Midori?

Midori (緑, meaning green) is a ceremonial matcha bar rooted in Japanese tradition. We believe preparing matcha is a ritual — not a transaction. Every detail communicates calm, craft, and connection.

MinimalIntentional CeremonialNatural

Stillness

Visuals breathe. Language is sparse. Space is generous. Midori never shouts — it invites.

Craft

From the grade of our matcha to the kerning of our type — nothing is accidental.

Connection

Matcha connects people to land, to ritual, to each other. Warm beneath the minimal surface.

Tone of Voice
Calm & precise
"A single bowl. Prepared with intention. Served without hurry."
Short, unhurried sentences. Let whitespace do work.
"Order your matcha now! Fast service, great taste!"
Knowledgeable, not exclusive
"Stone-ground to a 5-micron powder — the difference you taste."
Educate without gatekeeping. Everyone is welcome.
"Only true connoisseurs will appreciate this."
Sensory & specific
"The color of a forest after rain. The texture of still water just before it moves."
Appeal to sight, smell, touch. Be evocative but concrete.
"Delicious matcha with amazing flavor and great vibes."
Never: trendy, rushed, loud
"We don't do limited drops. Midori is always here when you need stillness."
No exclamation marks. No FOMO. No urgency language.
"Limited time only!! Don't miss out 🔥🔥"
02 — Logo

The mark of Midori

One wordmark. MIDORI set in Cormorant Garamond italic, with 緑 in Seal red as a permanent signature detail. No symbol. No icon. Just the name — and what it means.

Primary — Wordmark
MIDORI 緑

Amber is the accent

All CTAs, active states, key highlights. The single warm note in a cool-green world. Never as a full background.

Seal is a detail

Used only on 緑, tiny signature dots, and packaging seals. Never for buttons, links, or UI states. You notice it second — not first.

Slate is invisible

Muted gray-blue for captions, timestamps, metadata only. Never as a feature or accent color.

04 — Typography

Two voices, one brand

Cormorant Garamond carries emotion, history, and poetry. Outfit handles everything functional with clean precision.

Display — Cormorant Garamond
"A single bowl. Prepared with intention."

Headlines, hero statements, pull-quotes. Light (300) or Regular (400). Italics preferred. Use for emotion — never for instructions or UI labels.

Body — Outfit
ABCDEFGHIJK
abcdefghijk — 0 1 2 3 4

All body copy, UI labels, menus, captions. Geometric, highly legible at any size. 300 for body, 500 for labels, 600 for UI elements.
