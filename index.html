How I Work

My Process

I approach every project with both design intention and performance in mind.

01

Understand the Goal

Review campaign goals, audience needs, and performance context before touching a single pixel.

02

Identify Friction

Look for gaps in the user journey, messaging, or visual hierarchy that are costing conversions.

03

Design the Experience

Create layouts and creative that make the next step feel clear, natural, and visually compelling.

04

Launch & Measure

Support rollout, review results with GA4, and track what's working.

05

Optimize & Refine

Use data and feedback to improve performance over time. The work is never really done.

About Me

I'm a digital experience designer with a background in marketing and growth, focused on creating work that is both visually strong and performance-driven.

My experience spans landing pages, digital campaigns, ad creative, and conversion-focused design. I enjoy building digital experiences that connect the first impression of a campaign to the action that follows.
