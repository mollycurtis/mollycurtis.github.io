Introduction

Who is wander?

Solo travel for women is growing fast — wander was built for it.

"Travel alone. Not unsupported."

wander combines safety-reviewed itineraries, community-sourced stays, an offline companion app, and check-in tools that keep everyone informed. Planning that respects your intelligence and your independence. We don't warn. We equip.

EmpoweringSafety-first AdventurousHonest

Freedom by design

Does this give her more freedom, or less? If it adds friction without adding safety, it doesn't belong.

Vetted, not sponsored

Every stay reviewed by solo women travelers. No affiliate rankings, no paid placement.

Specific over safe

We name guesthouses, streets, and hours. Vague advice is useless advice.

Tone of Voice
Warm & direct
"This guesthouse is run by a local woman. Book it."

Like your most well-traveled friend. Confident, specific, no hedging.

"Users may wish to consider various accommodation options based on their preferences."

Empowering, not patronizing
"Bali solo is different than the blogs say. Here's the real picture."

Context, not warnings. Trust members to make good calls. Give the honest version, not the cautious one.

"Be careful!! Women traveling alone should always exercise extreme caution."

Community-voiced
"342 wander women stayed here. 97% would return."

Real numbers, real people. Community is the authority.

"Our expert travel editors recommend this highly-rated destination!"

Never anxious or corporate
"Go. We've done the groundwork."

Short, confident, specific. No "wanderlust." No exclamation marks. No "amazing."

"Embrace your wanderlust with our amazing female-focused travel experience! ✈️"

Do

Always italic, always lowercase, always Playfair Display. On Ember, Cream, or white. Keep the clear space. Let the name do the work.

Don't

Never all-caps, never upright, never in Gold or off-brand colors. Never on photo backgrounds.

04
Typography

Three voices, one clarity

Playfair for emotion. Syne for structure. DM Sans for function. Each has a lane.

Display — Playfair Display Italic
Go where you belong.

Hero headers, wordmark, pull quotes. Always italic, weight 400–700. The emotional register of wander — graceful and authoritative.

Aa Aa Aa
Label — Syne Bold
Vetted · Community · Safe

Nav, labels, tags, buttons. Uppercase, tracked. The structural layer — precise and forward without being clinical.

Regular Bold ExtraBold
Body — DM Sans
DM Sans 300 — long-form copy
DM Sans 500 — UI body

All body copy, app UI, captions. Clean and neutral. Never competes with Playfair. The quiet voice that carries the substance.

Never use
Playfair upright (non-italic)
Script or handwritten typefaces
System fonts (SF, Roboto, Arial)
A second serif near the wordmark
